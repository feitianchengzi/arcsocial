# Publish Checklist

## Content

- [ ] The post has one clear point.
- [ ] The opening line creates context or tension quickly.
- [ ] Claims are supported by experience, examples, references, or data.
- [ ] The ending tells the reader what to think about or do next.

## Platform

- [ ] The format matches the target platform.
- [ ] Length is appropriate for the platform.
- [ ] Links, tags, and mentions are handled correctly.
- [ ] Images or videos are attached where useful.

## Quality

- [ ] No placeholder text remains.
- [ ] No private notes or internal links are exposed.
- [ ] Tone matches `docs/voice.md`.
- [ ] Final version is archived after publishing.
